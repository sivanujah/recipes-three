BANANABREAD 

Ingrediens:
- 3 ripe Bananas
- 80 ml neutral oil 
- 110 g brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 200 g flour
- 3 ts backing powder
- 1 vanilla pod
- 1 dash cinnamon
- 1 dash of salt

1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees (fan oven: 160 degrees). Grease a loaf tin (inner dimensions 23 x 10 cm) well. Mash the bananas in a bowl with a fork.
2. Score the vanilla pod lengthwise with a knife and scrape out the seeds with the back of the knife. Whisk the oil with the brown sugar and eggs. 
3. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and vanilla pod and stir into the oil and egg mixture. Stir in the mashed bananas. Pour the batter into the tin and bake in the preheated oven for about 55 minutes. The banana bread makes approx. 10 pieces.
